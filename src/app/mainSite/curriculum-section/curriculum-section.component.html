MommaMia – Curriculum babies to 3 years

A well-structured, therapeutically designed school curriculum for babies and toddlers – 3 months to 3 years – that addresses all the
developmental spheres of the child’s life. As a qualified Occupational Therapist with more than a decade of experience in the field of
Paediatrics, Corien recognised the great need of parents to understand the principles of development and to gain the knowledge to apply these
enjoyable practices at home while treasuring moments of playful time with their children. Corien has conceptualised an educational programme,
developed with her treasured daughter Mia. Incredible Kids Daycare will be making use of this educational program with all our children aged
3 months to 3 years.

School Holidays

We open during the school holidays throughout the year but close for +/- three weeks over
the December holidays, from approximately 15 December to 11 January each year for our
annual shutdown (December fees are payable in full). Closed for public holidays.
