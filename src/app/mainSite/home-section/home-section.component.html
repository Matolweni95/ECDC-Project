WELCOME TO MOUNTAIN VIEW
CHILD CARE SOCIETY

Childrens light child education centre is a ECD(Early child development) school, founded
in 2015, situated in Claremont, Cape Town, and offers quality education from Grade R(0)
to Grade 3. The school has an outstanding progress record. In addition, Children's light
enjoys a fine reputation of offering immursive learning opportunities to help develop your
child whilst also having fun. The purpose of Children's light is and always has been to provide
quality education, building environments of learning and growth, moulding young minds for
a brighter future.

Covid 19

Children are incredibly adaptable and resilient. With positive guidance, they will adapt
and adjust to the social distancing measures that will be gently encouraged in classes and
playgrounds. Every safety and health aspect will be worked into the school day in the
most fun way possible. We do not want to scare our children. Hand washing will feel like
water play, going into situations where they need to wear their masks will make them feel
like superheroes, temperature checks will be fun. It is part of our profession to make
serious work feel like play. All staff have undergone excessive Covid training and we will
continue to keep abreast of any changes in this regards

“Mountain View will offer “Drop-in Childcare” – is an alternative to daycare.
Working parents and stay-at-home parents alike sometimes find that there are days
when a little extra help is needed. This program offers occasional, as-needed care for
those who may already have regular childcare, but find themselves needing additional
help because of school closings for school holidays, regular provider unavailability or
simply when you need some time to yourself or to pop into the office.
