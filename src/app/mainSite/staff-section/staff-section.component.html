Mountain View Child Care Society Staff are diligent and hard-working.

We have a dedicated teacher in each class who are asisted by our four supervisors and other assistants allowing us
to attend to each and every child’s needs. I also prioritise the training and development of my staff as I believe
that in order to get the best out of my staff, I need to ensure that they are properly trained and always equipped
to full-fill their daily tasks and responsibilities in excellence. Mountain View Child Care is always looking for
new ways to make learning and development fun. We set ourselves apart because our vision for all the children that
we have the privilege of teaching and caring for, is that we “Slow down, listen and learn, to see the world through
our children’s eyes”.
